How is a unique solution different from a template?

If you need a reliable author’s site, taking into account all the wishes and features, with the possibility of refinement, change of the functional part and design, then an individual solution is suitable for you. There are also types of sites and functional features that simply cannot be implemented on the template.

What does the project cost consist of?

Of course, out of man hours. Four types of specialists work on the project, namely: project manager, web-designer, front-end developer (responsible for the implementation of the site’s appearance), back-end developer (responsible for the implementation of the website’s functionality). A truly high-quality product can only be created by a qualified team consisting of the above specialists.

How is payment made? Do you have to pay all at once?

No, payment is done in stages. Usually there are five of them (together with an advance payment) and they are divided into approximately equal parts. It is worth noting that payment occurs only after the successful coordination of a specific stage with the customer.

Do you work under a contract?

Only under the contract, both with legal entities and individuals. Moreover, in the contract there are several applications that serve as supporting and regulatory documentation, for example, the terms of reference of the project.

I am afraid to tell an idea or disclose company private information.

We guarantee confidentiality, not only at the contract level, but also at the level of any negotiation rounds; if necessary, we sign a Non-disclosure agreement (NDA).
